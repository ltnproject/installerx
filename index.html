ⓘ Open this page on your iPhone or iPad. The IPA must be hosted on HTTPS and your device must trust the certificate.
// How it works
1
Paste your IPA's direct download URL and fill in the app details
2
Tap "Generate Install Link" — a manifest.plist is created and embedded in an itms-services:// URL
3
iOS prompts you to install the app. Confirm and wait for it to appear on your home screen
4
If prompted, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management to trust the developer certificate
